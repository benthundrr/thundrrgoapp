02 · The Posting Engine

Build a Consistency System

Volume at this level isn't grinding harder — it's a simple system you repeat every week. Aim for 3–5 posts a day.

🎥
Batch-film 2 days a week, post the other 5Two solid filming days can stock a full week of content.
🗂️
Post from a draft queue, never from scratch same-dayA queue means a busy day never breaks your streak.
⏰
Spread posts across 3 daily windowsRecommended: 6–9 AM · 12–2 PM · 7–10 PM local.
✦ Posting Cadence

Posting Cadence by Vertical

When you post depends on what you post. Hotels and attractions get booked on different rhythms — line your posts up with each one.

🏨 ACC · Hotels
Timing-driven
Post: Sunday evening → Wednesday night
Peak booking: Monday
Lever: STRONG — timing is a real, measured lever
Hotel bookings follow a weekly planning ritual — travelers research and book from a desk early in the work week, Monday being the single biggest reservation day. Post ahead of that window to be in front of the planner.
🎟️ TTD · Things To Do
Discovery-driven
Post: Wednesday night → Sunday morning
Peak booking: Thursday–Saturday (weekend surge)
Lever: SOFT — a secondary optimization, not the main driver
Tours and attractions get booked last-minute and in-destination — often same-day, 0–3 days out, on mobile while the traveler is already there. The real driver is evergreen discoverability: your content needs to be live and searchable when someone nearby is deciding "what do we do today." The Wed–Sun window rides the weekend surge on top of that base.
🧭
Hotels reward leading the planner. Attractions reward being there when they search.
03 · Retention

Win the First 3 Seconds

Most viewers decide to stay or scroll before second three. The hook is the highest-leverage edit you can make.

🎬
Open on your single best visual — the rooftop, the view, the revealNever open on a walk-up, a logo, or "hey guys."
💬
State the place + the payoff in the first line of on-screen text"$120 beachfront room in Pensacola" tells them why to watch.
✂️
Cut the intro — every second must earn its placeIn the data, longer videos lose completion. Tighten ruthlessly.
04 · Get Found

Caption & SEO Optimization

TikTok GO is a search engine — your caption is how buyers find you weeks after you post.

🔑 Lead with location
Open with the place name — it's your #1 keyword.
💲 Anchor the price
Add "under $150/night" or "starting at $89" to signal value.
⚡ Use action verbs
Book · Stay · Visit · Experience · Explore — words that convert.
👆 Close with a CTA
End every caption with "Link in bio" or "Tap to book."
05 · What the Data Shows

Captions Sell

The single biggest difference between posts that sold and posts that didn't wasn't the video — it was the caption length.

✕ Too short
~6 words
"Nice hotel 🏨 #travel" — what most posts look like. Rarely converts.
✓ Tells the story
~40+ words
"Beach AND pool views on the Emerald Coast? I loved my stay at the Fairfield Inn & Suites — link in bio to book." This style drove $1.4K.
✍️
Posts that sold ran ~250 characters vs ~95 for the rest. Lead with the destination in your first two lines, then end with a clear CTA.

07 · Market Selection

Find Your Overlooked Market

You don't need to travel — the underserved market is usually right outside the hotspot. Scout it like this:

🔎
Search the marketplace for properties near you with few existing postsLow post count = low competition = your opening.
🚗
Target mid-size towns & regional gems 30 minutes off the tourist stripThe boutique inn, the lake resort, the small-town stay.
📌
Pick 3–5 markets you can reach and own themRepeat visits build local knowledge and a recognizable lane.
08 · Start Measuring

Read Your Analytics

At L1 you start letting data steer you. Three numbers tell you what to do next:

👀 Views
Did it get reach? Low views = weak hook or wrong tags.
👆 CTR
Did people click? Low CTR = weak caption or no clear CTA.
💰 Orders
Did it sell? Views but no orders = fix the offer, not the camera.
🔁
Check weekly and replicate your top post — same hook, caption style, and market — instead of starting from scratch. This habit becomes your L2 review loop.
